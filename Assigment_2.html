KHREWS

Introduction

Khadim Hussain Educatinal and Welfare Service (KHREWS) is a non-profit organization. This service has been named on the Great Man, the Great Religious and Political Pakistani Leader named Allama Khadim Hussain Rizvi. He worked for Khatm-E-Nabuwat and Namoos-E-Risalat Peace Be Upon Him. This service acquires his name to follow the mission he gave.

Mission

Motto: WORKING FOR THE GLORY OF ISLAM & SERVING THE NATION.
